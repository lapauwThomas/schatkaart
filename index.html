About

Welcome to My Garden is a platform allowing citizens to put their garden freely at disposal as a rudimentary campsite to travelers. It's a not-for-profit initiative set up by a group of citizens.

You must follow at any time the government measures to stop the spread of Covid-19. You can find the latest information on info-coronavirus.be.



It looks like we will all be travelling in our own country this summer! While we often think of travelling as going far away, what would happen if we left to discover the unknown roads around us? What if this summer could make you discover Belgium in ways you’ve never done before?

This is the perfect time to discover what Belgium is all about, from Flanders to Wallonia passing through Brussels. Adventure begins at the end of the roads we know!

The source code of this project is available on GitHub. Any suggestions to make it better? Feel free to contribute!

Recommendations
Planning to discover Belgium? Here are some links and tools we'd recommend you to use!

  • Route planner for cycling and hiking: BRouter
  • Great navigation app for cycling and hiking: OSMAnd
  • Overview of the whole cycling network cycling layer of OpenStreetMap
  • Long distance trails in Wallonia: le RAVeL
  • Long distance trails in Flanders: LF network Flanders

Rules
Here’s how Welcome to My Garden works.
Make sure to read the rules before starting your adventure!

You must follow at any time the government measures to stop the spread of Covid-19. You can find the latest information on info-coronavirus.be.

Network of citizens
Welcome to My Garden is a network of citizens putting their private garden freely at disposal for you to camp. This is a non-for-profit initiative and no income should be made from it.

Disclaimer
As mentionned above, Welcome to My Garden is a citizen initiative. The volunteers behind Welcome to My Garden will not take any responsibility for any loss, damage or injury occurred with the use of the website and/or when staying in another citizen garden. By contacting a host on this platform, you aknowledge all the rules listed below, including the respect of the current COVID-19 measures.

Respect the COVID-19 regulation
Following the current government measures to stop the spread of the COVID-19, travelling in Belgium is currently not allowed. Welcome to My Garden requires all citizens on the platform to follow these measures. The initiative will not be held responsible in case some individuals disrespect the regulation.

Revocation option
If you have signed up as a garden camping, you have the right of withdrawal at any time. In this case, please send us a short email to welcometomygardennetwork[at]gmail.com.

Book in advance
Campground owners might not be home and ready to welcome you at all times. We ask users to book their campground in advance to check availability directly with the campground owners. This should be done by emailing or texting the campground owners (depending on their preference, you will find this information on the campground profile). When you book, add some information about you and at what time you’ll get there.

No car rule
Welcome to My Garden is an initiative that respects the environment which means you can only reach the campground by foot, bicycle or public transport. Getting there with your private car is forbidden.

Maximum length of stay
You can stay a maximum of 48hours on campgrounds.

Maximum amount of tents
Campground owners define how many tents they can accommodate each night (this is not Dour Festival). Please respect their choice.

Facilities
Facilities may vary from one campground to another. Some campground owners might offer access to their bathrooms and some others might prefer not to. Please respect their choice for this as well.

Respect for nature
All your waste has to be taken away. Respect nature and the campground owners.

No noise policy
Please be respectful of the campground owners by not singing Celine Dion too loud - or making too much noise - after 10pm!

Have fun!
This is a great opportunity to meet each other and have fun! Learn a few words of french, dutch or german if you’re travelling in a different region than yours. Be curious, share stories and make new friendships!

Share the word about Welcome to My Garden
Do you want to open your garden or know someone who would be happy to do it? Spread the word about the network!
